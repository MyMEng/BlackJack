This module contains dealer strategies configuration and predicates.

dealer(-Strategy) is det[private]
Defines the strategy played by the dealer (s17 is better for the player), either:
  • h17 - hit soft 17;
  • s17 - stand on ALL 17's.
Arguments:
Strategy- Strategy of the dealer: h17 or s17 as explained above.
croupierAI(-NNTable, -NNDeck, +NTable, +NDeck) is det
Plays defined dealer strategy based on dealer predicate. The dealer is always last in the queue to draw a new card.
Arguments:
NNTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NNDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) before dealer's decision.
NDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) before dealer's decision.
stand(-NNTable, -NNDeck, +NTable, +NDeck) is det[private]
Plays h17: hit soft 17, strategy. The dealer is always last in the queue to draw a new card.
Arguments:
NNTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NNDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) before dealer's decision.
NDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) before dealer's decision.
aceDillemaStand(-Decision, +Scores) is det[private]
Decide vale of hand - can be more than one with Ace (1 or 11 points) - based on current strategy and score. Decision based on stand strategy.
Arguments:
Decision- A score of a player chose based on current strategy.
Score- A list of possible score interpretation: there can be more than one if a player has Ace in his hand.
hit(-NNTable, -NNDeck, +NTable, +NDeck) is det[private]
Plays s17: stand on ALL 17's, strategy. The dealer is always last in the queue to draw a new card. If Ace & score <17 treat Ace as 11; otherwise if score >17 swap value of Ace to 1.
Arguments:
NNTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NNDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) before dealer's decision.
NDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) before dealer's decision.
aceDillemaHit(-Decision, -Type, +Aces, +Scores) is det[private]
Decide vale and type of hand - can be more than one with Ace (1 or 11 points)
  • based on current strategy, number of Aces in the hand and score. Decision based on hit strategy.
    @param Decision A score of a player chose based on current strategy. @param Type A type of played hand. @param Aces A number of aces in the hand. @param Score A list of possible score interpretation: there can be more than one if a player has Ace in his hand.
