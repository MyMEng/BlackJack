This module contains dealer strategies configuration and predicates.

croupierAI(-NNTable, -NNDeck, +NTable, +NDeck) is det
Plays defined dealer strategy based on dealer predicate. The dealer is always last in the queue to draw a new card.
Arguments:
NNTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NNDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) after dealer's decision.
NTable- A list of all player's cards (list of lists containing predicates card) before dealer's decision.
NDeck- A list of cards in the deck (list of predicates card) before dealer's decision.
